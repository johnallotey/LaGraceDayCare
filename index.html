Welcome To LaGrace Family

Incorporating a blend of an educational curriculum and the learning-through-play. This approach aims at promoting exploration and discovery in children, as well as reshaping their thought pattern to be thinkers and problem solvers. This approach helps them to better understand the world around them, i.e the environment, culture and society.

Montessori style

We value children as individuals, thus teaching them to be independent, self-confident and self-control over impulses and emotions.

Reggio approach

Will equally be used as a way for children to demonstrate ways in which they understand and learn through their thoughts and creativity.

Waldorf approach

emphasizes on imagination in learning and integrates holistically practical and artistic development.
